There are two ways to listen a music:

-You can load it directly from my site by clicking on the name of the song (it will take about 10~15 minutes) and after your Real Player will automatically play it back.

-You can OPEN manually your Real Player, GOTO the menu "File", GOTO the option "Open Location" and TYPE exactly the url which is shown near the name of the song.

-Você tambem pode ABRIR o seu Real Player manualmente, IR ao menu "File", IR à opção "Open Location" e DIGITAR exatamente a url que esta ao lado do nome da canção.
